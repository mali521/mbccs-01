Introduction

The aim of Mama Baby Care School is to prepare boys & girls students well equipped with scientific knowledge, who will face the challenges of the modern world in a befitting manner. It lays special emphasis on character building education convent style and infuses in its student all those qualities which lead them to become useful citizen of this Islamic republic.

Mama Baby Care School for Boys & Girls is a private institution and runs. On non-commercial and non-political basis. It has well equipped science labs and above all has The services of highly qualified and devoted teachers. It is housed in a spacious buildings situated in the heart of the Gurumandir town & abounded by big centers which is in the central most & easily accessible place in Karachi.
