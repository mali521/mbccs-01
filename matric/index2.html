Introduction

Mama Baby Care School (MBCS) offers a challenging world wide education, in English to children of all nationalities within the greater boundaries. Mama Baby Care School is a fully attributed and qualified institute which serves around 17 branches in Karachi, Pakistan. MBCS is one of the highly credible institute which moto is to provide a quality education to their candidates and making their students base from the grass hood level.

Our aim is to develop the unique potential of each students so that they will attain high level of competencies across a broad range of subjects, the knowledge, skills and attitudes of life long learning and be independent learners and ethical international citizens of a challenging and changing world.
